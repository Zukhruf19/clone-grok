Your home for Digital
Technologies education

Grok Academy is your home for Digital Technologies education offering classroom-ready online and unplugged resources.
Drawing on decades of experience in teaching digital technologies, we provide support through teaching resources,
professional learning, and curriculum guidance. As an Australian charity, we aim to empower the next generation with the
skills, knowledge, and dispositions they need to become the creators of tomorrow.
